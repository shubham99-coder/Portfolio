Folio — Online Bookstore Website

Folio is a modern, responsive online bookstore web application designed to provide readers with a simple and elegant way to browse, search, and purchase books. The platform features a clean, minimalist interface built around a warm color palette and classic serif typography, giving it a bookish, editorial feel that matches its purpose.

Users can explore a curated catalog of titles across multiple genres — including Fiction, Self-Help, Sci-Fi, Romance, Biography, and Mystery — using a dedicated search bar and quick-filter genre tags. Each book is presented as a card showing its cover, title, author, genre, price, and live stock availability, along with a one-click "Add to Cart" option for a smooth shopping experience.

The site also includes essential e-commerce functionality such as user authentication (Log In / Sign Up), a shopping cart, and dedicated navigation for browsing the full book collection — making it a functional front-end demonstration of a real-world online retail experience.
